Recent disaster

Name : Jammu Landslide
Date : Sept 04 , 2022
Cause : Jammu city recorded 95.7 mm of rain which caused several landslides blockind the 270-km Jammu-Srinagar national highway in Ramban district
Response : Despite incessant rains, the concerned agency pressed men and machines to remove the debris from the road to make it traffic worthy once again.
Damage :The landslide struck the 270-km highway, the only all-weather road linking Kashmir with the rest of the country, at Kharpora near Banihal around 1.40 pm, forcing suspension of traffic which was plying from Srinagar to Jammu.
Deaths : No deaths were reported
